About us:

NEW is an AI agent created by a team of engineers under the supervision of Dr. Keyvan Jafari Tehrani started on May 2023, on the 20th birthday of the JTC Company.

NEW uses cutting-edge AI, ML, statistical methods, and historical data on Iron ore for modeling the Market and predicting Iron ore prices.

This is our first release of NEW, on May 2024, to develop the market analysis using AI. Our goal is to offer a method for data-driven decision-making for the mineral trade industry. With that in mind, the team behind NEW is confident that we can make a positive impact on improving the mineral trade business by increasing human-market interaction. We believe that AI will help every company thrive and manage every aspect of mineral trade business.

Stay tuned for more of our model and application launches coming up!
